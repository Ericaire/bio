Selected Work

Publications and Manuscripts

InstantViR: Real-Time Video Inverse Problem Solver with Distilled Diffusion Prior

Weimin Bai*, Suzhe Xu*, Yiwei Ren, Jinhua Hao, Ming Sun, Wenzheng Chen, He Sun

arXiv 2025. CVPR 2026 Highlight.

Introduces a paired-data-free amortized inference framework that distills a video diffusion teacher into a causal one-step student for video inpainting, Gaussian deblurring, super-resolution, and text-guided reconstruction.

  • One-step solver for streaming video inverse problems.
  • Teacher-space regularized LeanVAE for low-latency decoding.
  • Over 35 FPS at 832 x 480 with up to 100x speedup.

Project arXiv

Bridged Posterior Distillation for Real-Time Video Inverse Problems

Suzhe Xu, Weimin Bai, Jinhua Hao, Ming Sun, Wenzheng Chen, He Sun

Manuscript in preparation. InstantViR++.

Builds on InstantViR by identifying three distillation gaps: causal information mismatch, latent-space mismatch, and objective mismatch. The framework combines CAI, BDC, and CLA to improve sharpness, training stability, and throughput.

  • CAI avoids posterior-mean collapse in causal students.
  • BDC reduces DiT tokens by 4x with zero inference overhead.
  • CLA improves PSNR by 4.5 dB over fixed-weight training.
